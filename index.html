About the Project

When a vehicle approaches, motion sensors detect it and automatically light up the next 20 meters of road. Lights behind the vehicle turn off to reduce unnecessary energy consumption.

How FLASH Works: Step by Step

Step 1: A car moves on the road
Step 2: When it reaches a specific pole (example: pole 4), the motion sensor detects the car
Step 3: Lights from pole 4 to pole 8 turn ON automatically
Step 4: Lights behind the car (poles 1 to 3) turn OFF
Step 5: The system always keeps 20 meters of light ahead of the vehicle
